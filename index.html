  • Receiver-paid gas fees
    Receiver-paid gas fees work by using complex cryptography to give someone permission to withdraw from your wallet without broadcasting anything to the blockchain. Receiver-paid gas fees work similarly to checks in the real world financial system. Before you can use receiver-paid gas fees for the first time, you need to approve a special smart contract to spend your tokens.
